Black Olive Ventures Pvt. Ltd. (BOV)

Black Olive Ventures (BOV) is a Research-Driven & Ethical Valuation services Company with over 15 years of experience across diverse asset classes.

Specializing in tangible asset valuation, including land, buildings, and plant machinery, BOV serves clients such as banks, non-banking financial companies (NBFCs), and housing finance companies (HFCs). BOV conducts valuations and provide support services for various purposes, including:

  • Mortgage Valuation & Loan Against property (LAP)
  • Construction Finance (CF)
  • Approved Project Finance (APF)
  • Lender’s Independent Engineering
  • Plant, Machinery & Inventory Valuations
