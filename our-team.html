At SFA, we are redefining trust and precision in valuation services. As a comprehensive valuation and consultancy firm, we specialize in offering innovative and cutting-edge solutions across a diverse range of industries, including banking, real estate, and manufacturing. .

Uniquely positioned, the team has witnessed multiple economic recessions, periods of property market decline and periods of recovery and expansion.

We believe that our knowledge of past cycles and various geographies provides us with an edge that translates into sound advice for our clients.

The management and top team have Cumulative experience of over 100 man years.

Mr. Sunil Agarwal

Founder & Managing Director

Sunil is a real estate professional with 30 years of experience. He is the founder director of Black Olive Ventures, a mortgage valuation services provider in India. Previously he co-founded and was the CEO at South Asian Real Estate (SARE), a Private Equity backed real estate development company. He has also served at senior positions with, ICICI Ventures, HSBC, DS Group, Colliers, Chesterton Meghraj and School of Real Estate as its associate dean and director.

Sunil has expertise across all domains and asset classes of real estate with strong track record of Turnaround Management and Start-ups. An industry mentor, thought leader, advisor & trainer, he was featured in the “Real People” section of 2010 annual edition of “Realty Plus” magazine.

Sunil has been part of various TV Shows and advisory committees of SEBI, CREDAI, GACS, SPA, CEPT, RICS and other institutions of repute. Currently, he is an Investment Committee Member of “Nisus BCD” real estate fund, board member of Nisus Finance AMC, Chairman of ITPI (Headquarter Building), “Council member” & “Faculty” at “Institution of Valuers,” and Convener of “International Chamber of Valuers”.

Sunil holds a degree in Civil Engineering, Masters in Urban Planning (Housing), MBA in Finance & Marketing, M.Sc. in Plant & Machinery Valuation and an Honorary Doctorate in Real Estate.
